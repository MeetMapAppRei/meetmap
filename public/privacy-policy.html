Meet Map Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Meet Map ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our app and website.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address, username, and account identifiers.
  • User content: event posts, comments, photos, updates, and reports.
  • Location data: device location if you enable location features (for "Near Me").
  • Technical data: basic logs required to operate, secure, and debug the service.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide core app features (create/discover events, maps, directions, saved events).
  • Authenticate accounts and maintain sessions.
  • Moderate content and process user reports.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and security.

3) Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by service providers that help us run Meet Map (for example hosting, authentication/database, and map providers).

4) Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. Some data may remain in backups for a limited period.

5) Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative safeguards. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections where supported.

6) Your Choices

  • You can edit/delete event content you own in the app.
  • To request account or data deletion, contact us at support@findcarmeets.com.

7) Children

Meet Map is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law).

8) Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

9) Contact
