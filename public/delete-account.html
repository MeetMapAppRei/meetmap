Meet Map Account Deletion

Updated: March 19, 2026

How to request account deletion

  1. Email support@findcarmeets.com from the email address associated with your Meet Map account.
  2. Use subject line: Delete my Meet Map account.
  3. We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before deletion.

What is deleted

  • Account profile and authentication access for that account.
  • User-created content associated with that account where technically feasible (events, comments, and related profile references).

What may be retained

  • Security, fraud-prevention, and legal compliance records as required by law.
  • Backup data for a limited retention period before automatic deletion.

Timeline

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.
